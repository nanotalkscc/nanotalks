Emerging Nano-Technologies and materials that will change the WORLD

A nanometer is 10^-9 times of a meter. In perspective the ratio between the Earth and the marble is the same ration between a meter and a nanometer. Nano-Technology is the science of ‘building things’ at these extremely small scale by arrangements of the fundamental building blocks atoms and molecules.

At this scale we would have more room for complexity, accuracy and evolution. It has the potential to cure chronic diseases, improve environment etc.

Innovation in NanoTech

Nano technology is more efficient, accurate and is hoped to improve our traditional methodology of dealing with different things.

In the future we would be able to produce more output with less input and our overall cost for producing nano-bots would also be very less.
